Tread a Path (work in progress)

When you travel a moderate distance along a path you know well, or a well established route, roll +wits.

On a strong hit, you arrive at your destination without issue. Take +1 momentum.

On a weak hit, you arrive at your destination, but suffer a minor setback. Pick one.
 * Envision a problem at your destination
 * Envision a problem that you bring with you to your destination
 * Your journey is grueling, Pay the Price

On a miss, you encounter a danger that must be dealt with. Once you have dealt with the problem at hand, Tread a Path again, and choose one before you roll.
 * Add +1
 * Treat a miss as a weak hit
